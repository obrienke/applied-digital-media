Milk Chocolate

Milk choloclate is a form of solid chocolate containing cocoa, sugar, milk.

Dark Chocolate

Dark choloclate is a form of chocolate made of cocoa solids, cocoa butter, and sugar.

Dark cholocalte without added sweetener is known as bitter chocolate, unsweetened cholocate, plain chocolate, or 100% chocolate.
